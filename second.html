About

An Indian actress and a model, Kriti Sanon was born on 27th July 1990 in New Delhi, India. Initially a model, she started her acting career with the Telugu psychological thriller, 1 Nenokkadine (2014). Sabbir Khan`s romantic action drama, Heropanti (2014) opposite Tiger Shroff was Kriti`s first outing in Bollywood, for which she bagged her first Filmfare award for Best Female Debut.
